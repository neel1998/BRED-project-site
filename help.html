FAQs

Frequently ask questions

Q.

Can I fill the questionnaire later?

Participants are strongly advised to fill the questionnaire and survey immediately after listening to the audio book.

Q.

I see a message asking to wait before the time slot begins.

Please check your registered email for the timing details of your slot. Next, make sure your system time is correct.

Q.

I see a password field to enter the study, but I don't have one.

If you completed the survey about participants, please check your email for the password to join the study. If you are not a compulsive doodler, we are sorry, but you are not suitable for this study.

Q.

Do I need some equipment to participate in the study?

Any decent headphones/earphones are enough for the prupose of this study. You can participant in this experiment on any device, mobile or desktop.
